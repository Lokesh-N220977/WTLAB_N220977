The Truth: Side by Side

Detailed breakdown of what you expect versus what you get.

Expectation

Easy exams, fast success, instant confidence.

Many believe that once they graduate or start a job, the hard part is over. They expect a linear path to success with minimal friction.

Reality

Pressure, skill gaps, and slow, consistent growth.

In truth, the learning curve is steep. Imposter syndrome is real, and growth happens through mistakes and persistence, not instant wins.
